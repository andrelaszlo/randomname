About

Naming your baby? Trying to come up with a Google+ pseudonym or the hero's name for your new novel? The possibilities are literally endless.

The names are randomly generated using a markov model, based on public data from the U.S. Census Bureau. This basically means that the program "knows" that a real name that starts with "Jes" might end in "sie" or "us".
